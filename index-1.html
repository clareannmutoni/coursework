Futurcity Chengdu (China)

Chengdu Future City (also known as Chengdu Future Science and Technology City) is a major, high-tech development project located in the eastern part of Chengdu, Sichuan province, China. It is designed to be a "Future City" that blends sustainable, nature-integrated architecture with innovation, technology, and urban living.
